Outfit Weather

An app mainly focus on weather forecast that can help people easily make decision about their daily outfits depending on the weather conditions.
Target User: People concerns about fashion, and have no sense of the degree Centigrade or Fahrenheit Degree. .

  • Learn more

Persona

Outfit Weather will contain a major function of recommending types of clothes that users would better wear depending on the weather conditions, including temperature, wind speed, air conditions, and so on. User will be able to personalized the style of clothes, even related to the certain brand of clothing in order to get a more specific recommendation.
