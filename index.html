OutFit Weather

Your peronal fashionalist with weather forecast

  • Get Started

    • ADS and Persona

    ADS:

    An app mainly focus on weather forecast that can help people easily make decision about their daily outfits depending on the weather conditions.

    Target User:

    People concerns about fashion, and have no sense of the degree centigrade or Fahrenheit Degree.

    Persona:

    Outfit Weather will contain a major function of recommending types of clothes that users would better wear depending on the weather conditions, including temperature, wind speed, air conditions, and so on. User will be able to personalized the style of clothes, even related to the certain brand of clothing in order to get a more specific

    Contents

    Information

    City information (name, time, picture) Weather information (temperatures, sky condition, pollution). A whole set of outfits recommendation including tops, buttons, shoes Add buttons to add on more cities Sign in page Personalize fashion preference （premium） Link to fashion website to related to certain products (collaborate with fashion companies and making money) personalize feeling settings (preference selections) Preference selections: leggings vs.long skirt in windy day…

    Application Redesign Project

    Using Adobe XD.

    Main page

    pain point: the main screen contains too much unnecessary function. Some are linked to other websites. The tab bars are crowded. According to HIG, “Include essential tabs only, and use the minimum tabs necessary for your information hierarchy” , tab bars contains less important functions.

    According to HIG, “Avoid crowding a navigation bar with too many controls”, there were too much function within navigation bar of the main screen.

    Redesign Main Page

    1. Reduce the menu to 3 instead of 1o.

    cancel “bookmark” and “weather”

    Using useful function (translator function) replace repeated info on other screens.

    • Details

    Resturant Page

    pain point: According to HIG, “Limit the number of segments to improve usability”, it is not a good idea to use segmented control to too much segmented controls to display categories of restaurants. Way of ranking the restaurants are not efficient.

    • Details

    Redesign Resturant Page

    Enhancement: 1. Move the categories of restaurants to “the up left picker, which originally was “All”.

    2. Add the bookmark at the navigation bar, and divide the bookmark into two separate pages- bookmark for restaurants and bookmark for subway, which can help user focus on their current task.
